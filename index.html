Why choose Nicer Tours for your days trips in Nice?

Because of our Tour Options:

Guaranteed Departure Option:

Ideal for those with little flexibility in their dates or those visiting the area outside of the high season.

Flexibility Option:

Ideal for those who visit during the high season or for those who have flexibility in their travel dates.

Since selling at a loss is illegal in France, Nicer Tours must operate at a profit, as must all businesses.

However, perhaps on the day you book your tour, you are the only customers. Individuals and couples generally run the task of having their tour cancelled in this case, since most tours have a break even point of 4 customers. Or worse - maybe the customer will be offered an alternative tour or a similar tour operated by another company, one of whom Nicer Tours cannot guarantee the quality.

Avoid these possibilities by opting for the Guaranteed Departure Option.

The Guaranteed Departure Option by charging you the customer what is the break even point for the tour in question. So, for example, if you are 3 and the break even point is 4, you’ll be charged for 4 people, but have the luxury of having a more personal tour… without the crush of a full minibus.

Since we only debit you for the Flexibility Option pice when you reserve this option, and any eventual balance once your tour is underway, you know exactly what you pay on the day of your tour. Indeed, you’ll be asked to confirm the number of passengers and thus the price you pay via an app as your tour is underway. So, should your tour meet the break even point, you won’t face extra charges.

When you reserve your tour, you’ll be charged the rate advertised here.

However, if the tour reserved is not fully booked on the day of your reservation you accept the risk that yourtour date changes, is cancelled or be asked to either join another tour or have your tour operated by another company.

Note that Nicer Tours have many partners, however, we cannot guarantee the quality of our partners’ tours.

When you book this option, you also accept that refunds are not possible if you decide that you are no longer able to accept these conditions. Of course, if for any reason Nicer Tours themselves cancel your tour (through force major, for example) you will be fully refunded.
