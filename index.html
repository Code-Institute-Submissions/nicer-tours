Tour Options:

Guaranteed Departure:

Ideal for those with little flexibility in their dates or those visiting the area outside of the high season.

Flexible Departure:

Ideal for those who visit during the high season or for those who have flexibility in their travel dates.

When you reserve your tour, you’ll be charged the rate advertised here.

Your tour is guaranteed never to be cancelled, because if, on the reserved tour date, the tour does not reach the break-even point, you accept paying the difference. If the tour has reached the break-even point for its operation, you pay no surcharge.*

On the day of your tour, you will confirm the number of passengers in total on your tour, agreed with your guide, via an app. Nicer Tours then debit you the difference.

*Generally, the break-even point is 4 passengers per tour.

When you reserve your tour, you’ll be charged the rate advertised here.

However, if the tour reserved is not fully booked on the day of your reservation you accept the risk that your tour date changes or is cancelled. Alternatively, you may be asked to either join another tour or have your tour operated by another company*.

*Note that Nicer Tours have many partners, however, we cannot guarantee the quality of our partners’ tours.
